
# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 19, 2025

YBOWS Social App (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app to manage content on Facebook, Instagram, and Threads.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may collect the following information:  
- **Account Information:** Name, email, profile picture, and user ID associated with your Facebook, Instagram, or Threads account.  
- **Content Data:** Posts, videos, comments, and other content you create or manage using our app.  
- **Usage Data:** Interaction with our app, such as login times, features used, and error reports.  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:  
- To help you manage and schedule content on Facebook, Instagram, and Threads.  
- To provide analytics and insights about your account activity.  
- To communicate important updates or notices regarding the app.  
- To comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse.  

---

## 3. Sharing of Information

We **do not sell or rent** your personal information. We may share your data only in the following cases:  
- With Facebook/Meta as required for app functionality.  
- With service providers who assist us in operating the app.  
- When legally required or to protect our rights and property.  

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.  

---

## 5. Your Rights

You may:  
- Access, update, or delete your personal information stored in our app.  
- Revoke permissions granted to our app at any time via your Facebook, Instagram, or Threads account settings.
